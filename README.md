# 💫 About Me

I’m a **Senior Unity Game Developer** and founder of **Aqua Games**, with **7+ years of experience** building mobile games using **Unity, C#, gameplay systems, optimization, monetization, and publishing pipelines**.

I’ve published **100+ mobile games** and worked with publishers and studios including **Voodoo, Homa, TapNation, CrazyLabs, Supersonic, BoomBit, Joypac, and MondayOff**. Several shipped titles reached **millions of downloads** and ranked in the **Google Play Top Charts**, including Top 50 rankings in 2022.

My work covers the full game development pipeline:

- Game prototyping and MVP development
- Gameplay mechanics and system architecture
- Puzzle, hyper-casual, runner, idle, simulation, and arcade games
- Mobile optimization for FPS, memory, load time, and battery usage
- Ads, monetization, analytics, and live-game iteration
- Store-ready releases and publishing support

I’m currently pursuing my **MS at ULB Brussels**, majoring in **Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science**. My current focus is combining **AI, analytics, and game development** to build smarter, more engaging, and more scalable game experiences.
